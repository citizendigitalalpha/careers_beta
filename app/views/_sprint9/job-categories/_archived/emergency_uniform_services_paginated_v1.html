Emergency and uniform services

Bailiff

Enforcement agent, enforcement officer, high court enforcement officer

Bailiffs visit properties to collect debts, serve a court document or give a notice or summons.

Border Force officer

Border Force officers secure the UK border by carrying out immigration and customs controls for people and goods entering the UK.

Criminal intelligence analyst

Intelligence officer

Criminal intelligence analysts find and analyse data to help detect and prevent crime, and to protect the security of the UK.

Firefighter

Fireman, firewoman

Firefighters save people and property from fire and other dangers, and give advice on fire prevention.

Forensic scientist

Forensic scientists prepare traces of physical evidence for use in courts of law.

Paramedic

Ambulance paramedic

Paramedics deal with emergencies, giving people life-saving medical help.

Police officer

Policeman, policewoman, police constable

A police officer keeps law and order, investigates crime and supports crime prevention.

Prison officer

Prison guard, prison warder

Prison officers supervise and rehabilitate inmates in prisons, remand centres and young offenders' institutions.

Probation officer

Probation officers supervise people serving community and prison sentences, and help them to stay away from crime.

Security officer

Security officers make sure that buildings, valuables and people are safe and secure.
