Tourism and hospitality

Hotel receptionist

Hotel receptionists make guests feel welcome, manage room bookings (reservations) and deal with requests from guests.

Kitchen assistant

Kitchen assistants do basic food preparation, make sure chefs have everything they need and keep the kitchen clean.

Tourist guide

Tour guide, Blue Badge guide

Tourist guides show visitors around places of interest like cities, historic buildings and art galleries.

Travel agent

Travel consultant

Travel agents book and plan business and leisure travel for customers.

Waiting staff

Waiting staff serve customers in restaurants and cafes by taking orders and payment, serving food and preparing tables.
