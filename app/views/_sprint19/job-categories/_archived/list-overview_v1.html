Health and care

Children's nurse

Children's nurses provide care for children and young people with acute or long-term health problems.

Dental nurse

Dental nurses help dentists to care for their patients, and may also carry out reception work and other tasks.

Dental hygienist

Oral health practitioner

Dental hygienists offer advice, information and treatments to prevent and treat tooth decay and gum disease.

Dentist

Dental surgeon, dental practitioner

Dentists diagnose and treat teeth and mouth problems, and work to prevent dental disease and promote oral health.

GP

General practitioner, doctor

General practitioners (GPs) are doctors who provide medical services to people in the community.

Hospital doctor

A hospital doctor diagnoses and treats illness and disease in patients admitted to hospital.

Mental health nurse

Mental health nurses work in hospitals and the community, to support people with a range of mental health issues.

Midwife

Midwives give care and support to pregnant women and their babies, before, during and after childbirth.

Occupational therapist

Occupational therapists help people overcome difficulties caused by physical or mental illness, accidents or ageing.

Pharmacist

Dispensing chemist, community pharmacist, hospital pharmacist

Pharmacists provide expert advice on the use and supply of medicines and medical appliances.

Psychologist

Psychologists study people's behaviour, motivations, thoughts and feelings, to help them overcome or control their problems.

Psychotherapist

Psychotherapists use talking techniques and therapies to help people who are distressed, or have mental health problems.

Physiotherapist

Physiotherapists work with patients to improve their range of movement and promote health and wellbeing.

Radiographer

Medical radiographer

Radiographers use equipment to diagnose or treat patients who are ill or injured.

Play therapist

Play therapists help children to make sense of difficult life experiences or complex psychological issues through play.

Care worker

Care assistant, support worker, nursing home assistant

Care workers help vulnerable people to manage their daily activities and to live as independently as possible.

Childminder

Childminders care for babies, toddlers and children in their own home, supporting their learning and development.

Family support worker

Family support workers help and advise families with long- or short-term difficulties.

Nursery manager

Nursery assistant, pre-school assistant, playgroup assistant, nanny, childcare worker

Nursery workers help babies and children up to 5 years old to develop and learn in a safe and supportive environment.

Social work assistant

Community support worker, home care officer, social services assistant

Social work assistants give advice, guidance and support to help people with their physical, emotional and social needs.

Social worker

Social workers help to protect vulnerable children and adults from harm or abuse, and support people to live independently.

Bailiff

Enforcement agent, enforcement officer, high court enforcement officer

Bailiffs visit properties to collect debts, serve a court document or give a notice or summons.

Border Force officer

Border Force officers secure the UK border by carrying out immigration and customs controls for people and goods entering the UK.

Criminal intelligence analyst

Intelligence officer

Criminal intelligence analysts find and analyse data to help detect and prevent crime, and to protect the security of the UK.

Firefighter

Fireman, firewoman

Firefighters save people and property from fire and other dangers, and give advice on fire prevention.

Forensic scientist

Forensic scientists prepare traces of physical evidence for use in courts of law.

Paramedic

Ambulance paramedic

Paramedics deal with emergencies, giving people life-saving medical help.

Police officer

Policeman, policewoman, police constable

A police officer keeps law and order, investigates crime and supports crime prevention.

Prison officer

Prison guard, prison warder

Prison officers supervise and rehabilitate inmates in prisons, remand centres and young offenders' institutions.

Probation officer

Probation officers supervise people serving community and prison sentences, and help them to stay away from crime.

Security officer

Security officers make sure that buildings, valuables and people are safe and secure.
